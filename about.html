About Us

AFD Trading was conceptualised in 2002. AFD Trading, Jamaica's retailers for over 20 years, bring the best and latest advance technology to your home and office. When it comes to experience, no one can beat us! We are "Jamaica's Tech-Geeks"! Giving Jamaica years of excellent customer service, our team is always available to answer any questions with a smile:-) AT AFD TRADING, WHEN YOU ARE HAPPY, WE ARE HAPPY! Quality Guaranteed!

AFD Trading is one of the largest computer stores in Jamaica. We have been in business for twenty (20) years. The business evolved out of an undying love for technology and gadgets. Our main office is conveniently located at Golden Springs, Stony Hill, St. Andrew. We recently added our second location which is situated at Suite 17 Building C, Constant Spring, Kingston.

AFD Trading is the home to an extensive range of smartphones, tablets,desktops, smart TVs, microwave as well as home entertainment systems. For items we may not have available on hand, we offer a special order service. Details of this service can be provided by our customer service ambassadors. Our trained and knowledgeable staff provides excellent before and after sales support to all our customers.
